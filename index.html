What is app?

This is a place for you to do nothing for a bit, when you start the timer, you should not press any keys or move the mouse, or else the timer will end and restart. Living in a world filled with so much stimulation, it's so difficult to stop and do nothing to allow brains to just flow, so set up the time, click on the timer and just sit there. Let your mind wander.
